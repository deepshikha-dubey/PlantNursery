Service

Plant nurseries offer a range of services to gardeners, landscapers, and plant enthusiasts. Here are some common services provided by plant nurseries:

Landscaping

Design: in this nursery offer landscape design services, helping customers plan their outdoor spaces. Installation: assist with planting, hardscaping, and creating garden beds.

Lighting

Refer to the use of artificial lighting to support plant growth. While natural sunlight is ideal, artificial lighting becomes necessary in certain situations.

Hardscape

Hardscaping in a plant nursery refers to the non-living elements used in landscaping. These features are constructed from hard materials such as stone, concrete, brick, and metal. Here are some common hardscaping elements. Patios, Walkways, Retaining Walls, Decks, Pergolas

Watering

Watering is a critical aspect of plant care in nurseries. Here are some key points: Root Watering, Lifting Pots, on timing(Early morning and evening), Container Moisture Check

Irrigation

Irrigation plays a crucial role in maintaining healthy plants in a nursery. Here are some key aspects of nursery irrigation: Water storage and control, Drip irrigation, Sprikler irrigation

Outdoor Structures

Outdoor structures play a vital role in enhancing the aesthetics and functionality of a plant nursery. Metal Garden Arches:These elegant structures add vertical interest and can support climbing plants like roses or clematis. They create inviting entrances or pathways within the nursery
